Intro

A creative fellow of the games programming and game design variety that is actively pursuing ways to express and share my vision within the games industry. The field of study that particularly interests me is that of accessibility as I want to accommodate to those who cannot enjoy games to the same extent as anyone else as such researching ways to help to lessen this inconvenience was my number one priority, as it ensures that I have them in mind in every step of development. After all, as this quote from late Satoru Iwata suggests "Video Games are meant to be one thing. Fun. Fun for everyone".
